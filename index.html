For You ❤️

My intentions with you are pure.

I don’t want to hurt you — I want to make you smile.

I want you to know how special you are to me.

I won’t be someone who breaks your trust.
I will be someone you trust deeply,
someone you can share everything with
without fear.

I will never make you regret
letting me become close to you.

I’m sorry if someone before
made you feel small
when you loved them fully.

I just want to be your comfort.
Your peace.
The place your heart feels safe.

I don’t want anything from you —
except your happiness.

I promise.
